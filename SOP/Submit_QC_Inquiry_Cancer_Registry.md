### 1 Gather information from PI
Ask the PI for specific examples of their claim(s) that we can use to troublehoot.

---
### 2 Confirm Data Files are accurate
Look at our version of their dataset and confirm that there have been no errors in the data processing: extraction, sharing, PI loading/analyzing their files, etc...

---
### 3 Review Data
There are two levels for this review depending on the claim (Using Care Connect is fine for these cases). Sometimes, we might be able to look the patient's chart and determine, if what we have delivered corresponds with what's there and the explanation to what the PI is asking about and how to resolove it.

In other cases, we might need an expert opinion and shall ask the Director of the Consultants to help us out. Connect with her and be ready to share the sample data using Box and coordinate a session to review together as need.

---
### 4 Escalate Inquiry to Cancer Registry Team
If the review uncovers inconsistencies in the data that need to be addressed by the Cancer Registy team. 
- We shall open an ISS ticket containing a brief description of the issue. 
- The ticket itself shall not include PHI.
- We will put together a data file with the necessary information and copy it to the Box folder for the CT team to review.
- We shall create a subfolder for each request with the ticket# and the IRB for the project.
- Finaly, we will email the CR representative with the ticket # and folder name for their review.

ISS ticket: https://uclahsprod.service-now.com/now/nav/ui/classic/params/target/%24pa_dashboard.do

Box folder: https://uclahs.app.box.com/folder/367956877275

 ---
 ### 5 Manage PI Communications and Expectations
 Inform the PI that we have activated our protocol and are requesting help to the Cancer Registry team to address the issue that they have raised.

---
### FAQs
- You shall also open a sub-item in Monday.com (type: data inquiry) to track this activity as well.
- The time spent on this shall be documented as well.
